# EcoRisk Nexus – SME Risk & Sustainability Dashboard  
**Category:** Strategy / Sustainability / Business Intelligence  

---

## Business Problem  
Small and mid-sized enterprises (SMEs) lack accessible tools to assess operational, financial, and sustainability-related risks in a single, integrated view.

---

## Why It Matters  
Early risk detection enables SMEs to:
- Make proactive, data-driven decisions  
- Remain compliant with evolving ESG expectations  
- Build long-term resilience  
- Reduce dependence on heavy manual processes  

---

## Approach  
- Designed an end-to-end **risk assessment dashboard**  
- Integrated structured datasets using **ETL pipelines**  
- Built KPI frameworks to track operational and sustainability risk indicators  
- Visualized executive-level insights using **Tableau**  

---

## Impact & Key Insights  
- Faster identification of high-risk operational and sustainability areas  
- Improved decision-making efficiency  
- Consolidated fragmented risk signals into a single analytical view  

---

## Tools & Technologies  
- Tableau  
- SQL  
- Excel  
- ETL Pipelines  
- KPI Framework Design
